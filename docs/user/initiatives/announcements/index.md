Announcements let initiative owners share updates with backers directly on the initiative's public page — progress reports, milestones, or news about how funds are being used.

## Before you begin

Your initiative must be approved and published. You must be signed in as the initiative owner with your [LF ID](https://openprofile.dev/).

## Posting an announcement

Announcements are created and managed from **LFX Self Serve**, not from the public LFX Crowdfunding site.

1. Go to [My Initiatives on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/initiatives) and select your initiative.
2. Open the **Announcements** tab.

   ![Self Serve announcements tab](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/announcements-self-serve-tab.png)

3. Select **New Announcement** and provide:
   - **Title**
   - **Description** — supports rich text formatting

   ![New announcement form](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/announcements-self-serve-form.png)

4. Select **Publish**.

You can edit or delete an announcement at any time from the same tab.

## How announcements appear to backers

Published announcements are shown in a timeline on the initiative's public page, newest first, each with its title, description, and post date.

![Announcements timeline on initiative page](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/announcements-public-timeline.png)

There is no limit on the number of announcements shown — all published announcements for the initiative appear in the timeline.
