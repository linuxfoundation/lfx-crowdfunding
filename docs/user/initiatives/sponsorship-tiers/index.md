Sponsorship tiers let an initiative offer a fixed set of giving levels — such as **Platinum**, **Gold**, **Silver**, and **Bronze** — each with its own goal amount and benefits, instead of accepting only open, any-amount donations.

## Setting up tiers during initiative creation

When creating an initiative, choose **Fixed tiers** on the **Donation options** step of the creation form.

![Donation options step — choose fixed tiers](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/sponsorship-tiers-create-mode.png)

For each tier you want to offer, set:

- **Enabled** — toggle a tier on or off
- **Goal amount** — the donation amount associated with the tier
- **Benefits** — a list of what a sponsor at that tier receives (e.g. "Logo on homepage"); select **+ Add benefit** to add more

![Configuring a sponsorship tier](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/sponsorship-tiers-create-form.png)

Tiers are always named **Platinum**, **Gold**, **Silver**, and **Bronze**; only the goal amount, benefits, and whether the tier is enabled can be customized. If you choose **Open donations** instead, backers can give any amount and no tiers are shown.

## Editing tiers after publishing

Sponsorship tiers can be updated at any time from **LFX Self Serve**:

1. Go to [My Initiatives on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/initiatives) and select your initiative.
2. Open the **Sponsorship Tiers** tab.

   ![Self Serve sponsorship tiers tab](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/sponsorship-tiers-self-serve-tab.png)

3. Update goal amounts, benefits, or enable/disable tiers, then select **Save**.

Changes are reflected on the public initiative page and donation flow immediately.

## How tiers appear to donors

When a donor opens the **Donate** drawer on an initiative with fixed tiers, each enabled tier is shown as a card with its badge, goal amount, and benefits list.

![Sponsorship tiers shown in the donation flow](/images/docs/screenshots/donations/sponsorship-tiers-donate-drawer.png)

Selecting a tier sets the donation amount to that tier's goal amount. Donors can also skip the tiers and choose **a custom amount** below the tier cards.
