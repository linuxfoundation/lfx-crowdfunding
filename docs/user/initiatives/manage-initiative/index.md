Managing your initiative lets you update its details and track financial activity after it has been approved and published on LFX Crowdfunding.

## Before you begin

Your initiative must be approved and published. You must be signed in as the initiative owner with your [LF ID](https://openprofile.dev/).

## Steps

1. Go to [My Initiatives on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/initiatives).

    ![My Initiatives list](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/manage-initiative-list.png)

2. Select the initiative you want to update.

    ![Initiative edit form](/images/docs/screenshots/initiatives/manage-initiative-edit.png)

3. Update any of the following fields and select **Save**:
   - Name, description, and logo
   - Funding goal
   - Beneficiaries
   - Fund distribution

## Financial reporting

All income and expenses are visible on your initiative's **Financials** tab on LFX Crowdfunding. This includes:

- Total funds raised
- A breakdown of donations by supporter
- Approved expenses by category

Financial data is automatically synced from the Linux Foundation's ledger system.

## After completing

Your changes are reflected on the public initiative page immediately. Financial data updates automatically — no manual action is required.
