Once your initiative is approved and published, you can manage it through the LFX platform. Visit [My Initiatives on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/initiatives) to update your initiative's details.

## What you can update

From the LFX platform you can update your initiative's name, description, logo, funding goal, beneficiaries, and fund distribution at any time.

## Financial reporting

All income and expenses are visible on your initiative's **Financials** tab on LFX Crowdfunding. This includes:

- Total funds raised
- A breakdown of donations by supporter
- Approved expenses by category

Financial data is automatically synced from the Linux Foundation's ledger system.
