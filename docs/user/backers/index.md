Backers are the donors and sponsors publicly listed on an initiative page after making a contribution to an open source campaign on LFX Crowdfunding.

Every initiative publicly recognises the people and organisations that have contributed to it. Backers are listed in two places on each initiative page.

## Recent donations sidebar

![Recent donations sidebar](/images/docs/screenshots/backers/backers-recent-sidebar.png)

The initiative overview shows the most recent donations in a sidebar, including each backer's name, avatar, amount, and how long ago the donation was made.

## Financials tab

![Financials tab donations table](/images/docs/screenshots/backers/backers-financials-tab.png)

The **Financials** tab contains a full donations table with each backer's:

- Name and avatar
- Donor type — **Individual** or **Company**
- Donation amount
- Date

Select **Load more** at the bottom of the table to see older donations.

## Anonymous donations

If a donor's information is not available, their name is shown as **Anonymous**. There is no way to filter or exclude anonymous donations from the list.

## After donating

Once your payment is processed your name and avatar appear on the initiative's backer list. See [Making a Donation](../donations/make-donation/) for the full payment steps.
