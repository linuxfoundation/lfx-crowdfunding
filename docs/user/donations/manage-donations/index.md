Managing your donations lets you view your giving history and cancel any active monthly subscriptions on LFX Crowdfunding.

## Before you begin

You must have completed at least one donation. Sign in with your [LF ID](https://openprofile.dev/) before proceeding.

## Steps

1. Go to [My Donations on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/donations).

### View your donation history

![Donation history list](/images/docs/screenshots/donations/manage-donations-list.png)

2. Review your past donations. Each entry shows the initiative name, amount, date, and payment status.

### Cancel a recurring donation

![Cancel subscription](/images/docs/screenshots/donations/manage-donations-cancel.png)

3. Find the active monthly subscription you want to cancel.
4. Select **Cancel** next to the subscription.
5. Confirm the cancellation when prompted.

## After completing

Cancellation takes effect immediately — you will not be charged again. The subscription will no longer appear in your active subscriptions list.
