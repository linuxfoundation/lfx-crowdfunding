Answers to the most common questions about donating on LFX Crowdfunding.

## Is there a minimum donation amount?

There is no enforced minimum for one-time donations. Monthly recurring donations must be at least $1 per month.

## Do I need an account to donate?

Yes. You must be signed in with an [LF ID](https://openprofile.dev/) account to complete a donation.

## Are transaction fees deducted from my donation?

No. The Linux Foundation underwrites all transaction fees, so 100% of your donation goes to the initiative.

## Can I donate anonymously?

LFX Crowdfunding does not currently offer a donor-selectable anonymous option. Your name and avatar will appear on the initiative's backer list. If your donor information is unavailable at the time of processing, your name will appear as **Anonymous**.

## How do I cancel a monthly donation?

Go to [My Donations on LFX](https://app.lfx.dev/crowdfunding/donations), find the active subscription, and select **Cancel**. Cancellation takes effect immediately and you will not be charged again. See [Managing Your Donations](../manage-donations/) for full steps.

## Can I get a refund?

Donations are processed immediately and are generally non-refundable. If you believe a charge was made in error, contact the Linux Foundation support team.

## Is my donation tax-deductible?

All donations are made to the Linux Foundation, a 501(c)(6) non-profit organisation (a US non-profit category). Tax deductibility depends on your jurisdiction — consult your tax adviser.

## Why was my card declined?

Common reasons include incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or a 3D Secure (an extra authentication step your bank may require) challenge that was not completed. Try re-entering your card details or contact your bank.
